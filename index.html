Who are KGLW?

King Gizzard & the Lizard Wizard are an Australian rock band formed in 2010. They are known for their wild variety of music genres across their plathora of music albums. From psychedelic garage rock and jazz-fusion in the early 10's to heavy metal, synth-pop and even an orchestral rock album as their full album. The biggest throughline of all their works is their willingness to experiment, as well as the 'Gizzverse'; a fictional universe where most of their songs' story take place in. King Gizzard & the Lizard Wizard's discography often feature enviromental themes, as well social and political issues.
